Installation steps:

  1. Upload the application folder to your root directory
  2. Open application/controllers/SocialLogin.php and update your Facebook and Google application credentials
  3. Go to phpMyAdmin and upload the user.sql file
  4. Now go to the login or signup page and you can see the Facebook and Google login options

Follow these steps to seamlessly integrate Social Login into your application.
